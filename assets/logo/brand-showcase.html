The Concept

A listening pulse. A solid core orb sits at the center — Milo, present and ready. Sound waves pulse outward on both sides: the universal signal of a voice assistant actively listening and speaking.

The mark is built from one orb and four arcs — calm, friendly, and instantly legible from a 16px favicon up to an app icon. The outer waves fade, suggesting sound radiating into the room: hands-free, even in CarPlay.
