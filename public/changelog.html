1.0.0

TestFlight beta

June 2026 · iOS 17.6+

First public beta: hands-free Siri to the model you choose, with CarPlay-friendly answers and on-device history.

Added

  • Siri bridge — “Hey Siri, ask MILO…” from lock screen, headphones, or CarPlay.
  • Eight providers — OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, Mistral, Groq, OpenRouter (200+ models), Apple Intelligence, and local Ollama.
  • Voice model switching — change provider or model mid-conversation without opening the app.
  • CarPlay mode — shorter, spoken answers tuned for driving.
  • On-device history — searchable threads with tokens, latency, and estimated cost per conversation (SwiftData).
  • Provider settings — per-model temperature, max tokens, and system prompts.
  • API key management — keys stored locally and encrypted in the iOS Keychain.
  • Help & Guide — built-in Siri phrase examples and sidebar navigation.
